Gauhati University
Information Technology






Vision & Mission

Vision

To establish a structured seat of learning for inclusive, adaptive and cutting-edge education, training and research in the field of Information Technology and allied domains that would empower the North-Eastern region and beyond.


Mission

  • To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure and systematically advanced programmes at undergraduate, postgraduate and research levels in IT, Information Science, Computer Science and related disciplines.

  • To introduce contemporary specialisations such as AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, IoT, Brain-Computer Interface, Cyber-Security, Image & Speech Processing, etc., thereby aligning curriculum with evolving technology trends.

  • To build human- and technological-resources for the region, fostering innovation, high-quality research and employability in national and international arenas.
