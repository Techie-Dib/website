Welcome to IT Department

The Department of Information Technology (renaming the Department of Computer & Information Technology in June 2012) under the Faculty of Technology, GU has been established to facilitate a state-of-the-art infrastructure for different Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Research programs in the field of Computer and Information Technologies.

RegICON 2025

Regional International Conference on Natural Language Processing

About the Conference

RegICON 2025 is a premier international conference focusing on Natural Language Processing advancements in East India, North East India, and Southeast Asia.

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission: September 15, 2025
  • Acceptance Notification: October 20, 2025
  • Camera Ready: November 10, 2025
  • Conference Dates: November 27-29, 2025

Call for Papers

We invite original research papers, case studies, and review papers on all aspects of Natural Language Processing.
